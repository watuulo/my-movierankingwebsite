Presumed Innocent

Presumed Innocent (1990) is a gripping legal thriller directed by Alan J. Pakula and based on the best-selling novel by Scott Turow. The film stars Harrison Ford as Rusty Sabich, a prosecutor who finds himself accused of the murder of his colleague Carolyn Polhemus (played by Greta Scacchi). As Rusty battles to prove his innocence, dark secrets and complex relationships come to light, blurring the lines between guilt and innocence. With a riveting plot, strong performances, and an unforgettable twist, Presumed Innocent keeps audiences on the edge of their seats until the very end.

Mad Max: Furiosa

Mad Max: Furiosa is an upcoming action-packed prequel to the highly acclaimed Mad Max: Fury Road. Directed by George Miller, the film delves into the origins of Furiosa, a character immortalized by Charlize Theron in Fury Road. The story follows a young Furiosa, played by Anya Taylor-Joy, as she navigates a desolate and dangerous wasteland, facing formidable adversaries and forging alliances in her quest for survival and justice. With stunning visuals, intense action sequences, and a compelling narrative, Mad Max: Furiosa promises to expand the legendary Mad Max universe and offer fans a deeper insight into one of its most iconic characters.
